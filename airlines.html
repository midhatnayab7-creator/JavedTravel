PIA

Pakistan International Airlines (PIA)

National Flag Carrier of Pakistan | IATA: PK | ICAO: PIA

About

Pakistan International Airlines is the national flag carrier of Pakistan. Founded in 1946, PIA operates the largest domestic network in Pakistan, connecting major cities including Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Peshawar, Quetta, and northern areas like Gilgit and Skardu.

Domestic Destinations

  • ✈ Karachi (Jinnah International)
  • ✈ Lahore (Allama Iqbal International)
  • ✈ Islamabad (New Islamabad International)
  • ✈ Peshawar (Bacha Khan International)
  • ✈ Quetta (Quetta International)
  • ✈ Multan (Multan International)
  • ✈ Faisalabad, Sialkot, Sukkur
  • ✈ Gilgit, Skardu, Chitral, Turbat, Gwadar

Fleet & Services

  • 🛫 Airbus A320 (Domestic)
  • 🛫 Boeing 737 (Domestic)
  • 🛫 ATR 72 (Northern Areas)
  • 🍽 Complimentary Meals on Select Routes
  • 💼 20kg Checked Baggage
  • 💻 Online Check-in Available

Airblue

Pakistan's Leading Private Airline | IATA: PA | ICAO: ABQ

About
