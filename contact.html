Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How do I book a domestic flight?

You can book through our website booking form, call us at 0318-1132286, or send your travel details via WhatsApp. We will confirm availability and share the fare details.

Which airlines do you offer tickets for?

We offer tickets for all Pakistan domestic airlines: PIA, Airblue, Serene Air, Air Sial, and Fly Jinnah. We compare prices from all airlines to get you the best deal.

Are flight timetables updated daily?

Yes, our flight timetables are updated daily based on airline schedules, seat availability, and booking status. Visit our Timetable page for the latest schedules.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers (all Pakistani banks), JazzCash, EasyPaisa, cash payment at office, and cheque payment.

Do you provide visa services?

Yes! We offer complete visa processing services for UAE, Saudi Arabia, Turkey, Malaysia, Thailand, UK, USA, Canada, and Schengen countries. Contact us for free consultation.

Can I cancel or change my booking?

Cancellation and change policies depend on the airline and fare type. Please contact us at 0318-1132286 for cancellation or changes to your booking.
