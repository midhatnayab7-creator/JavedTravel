Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a domestic or international flight?

Fill in our Flight Booking Form on this page, call us at 0318-1132286, or send your details via WhatsApp. We confirm availability and share fare details within 30 minutes.

Which airlines do you offer tickets for?

All Pakistan domestic airlines: PIA, Airblue, Serene Air, Air Sial, and Fly Jinnah. Internationally: Emirates, Etihad, Qatar Airways, Turkish Airlines, Saudia, Flydubai, Air Arabia, and more.

What payment methods do you accept?

Bank transfers (all Pakistani banks), JazzCash, EasyPaisa, cash payment at office, and cheque payment.

Do you provide visa services?

Yes! Complete visa processing for UAE, Saudi Arabia, Turkey, Malaysia, Thailand, UK, USA, Canada, and Schengen countries — with free consultation included.

Can I cancel or change my booking?

Cancellation and change policies depend on the airline and fare type. Contact us at 0318-1132286 to discuss your options.
